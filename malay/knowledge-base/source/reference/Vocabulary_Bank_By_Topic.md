# PSLE Malay Language — Vocabulary Bank by Topic
> For P6 PSLE 2026 preparation
> Use for: Karangan (Paper 1), Oral (Paper 4), Comprehension (Paper 2)

---

## How to Use This Bank

For **composition (karangan):** Use richer vocabulary instead of simple words.
For **oral:** Use topic-specific vocabulary when answering questions.
For **comprehension:** Recognise words in context and understand synonyms.

**Study method:** Cover the English column → read Malay → recall meaning. Then reverse.

## SECTION I: USEFUL LINKING & TRANSITION WORDS

## Flash Card Practice Format

For each word, create a flash card:

**Front:** Malay word
**Back:** English meaning + example sentence

**Example:**
- Front: *terharu*
- Back: moved/touched emotionally | "Air matanya bergenang terharu mendengar berita itu."
