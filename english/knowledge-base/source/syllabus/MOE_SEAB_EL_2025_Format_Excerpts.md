## Examination structure (200 marks total)

| Paper | Marks | Duration | Weighting |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 — Writing | **50** | 1 hour 10 minutes | 25% |
| Paper 2 — Language Use & Comprehension | **90** | 1 hour 50 minutes | 45% |
| Paper 3 — Listening Comprehension | **20** | approx. 35 minutes | 10% |
| Paper 4 — Oral Communication | **40** | approx. 10 minutes per student | 20% |
| **Total** | **200** | | 100% |

---

## Paper 1 — Writing (50 marks)

### Part 1: Situational Writing — 14 marks

- Length: ~150 words.
- Task: a functional piece (formal letter, informal email, report, speech, **article** (new in 2025), notice, review, recount, diary entry) based on a given stimulus (poster, advertisement, notice, etc.).
- **Six content points** must be addressed. **One of the six** is the "critical thinking" or "expository" point: the information for that point is **not in the stimulus** and must be generated by the student.
- Marking: Task Fulfilment (TF) + Language & Organisation (L&O). The 2025 revision reduced L&O by 1 mark.

### Part 2: Continuous Writing — 36 marks

- Length: at least 150 words; aim for 200–300 words.
- Task: narrative composition. Students choose **one of three** topic options, each accompanied by a picture.
- Marking sub-bands: Content / Language / Organisation.

## Paper 2 — Language Use & Comprehension (90 marks)

### Booklet A (25 marks, MCQ)

| Component | Items | Marks |
|---|---|---|
| Grammar MCQ | 10 × 1m | 10 |
| Vocabulary MCQ | 5 × 1m | 5 |
| Vocabulary Cloze | 5 × 1m | 5 |
| Visual Text Comprehension | 5 × 1m | 5 |
| **Booklet A total** | | **25** |

### Booklet B (65 marks, open response)

| Component | Items | Marks |
|---|---|---|
| Grammar Cloze | 10 × 1m | 10 |
| Editing for Grammar & Spelling | 10 × 1m (5 grammar + 5 spelling, indicative) | 10 |
| Comprehension Cloze | 15 × 1m | 15 |
| **Synthesis & Transformation** | 5 × 2m | **10** |
| Comprehension Open-Ended | ~10 questions over **one passage** (narrative *or* non-narrative, 500–600 words) | 20 |
| **Booklet B total** | | **65** |

### Notes

- **Synthesis & Transformation IS retained** in the 2025+ syllabus (10 marks). Common patterns: conjunctions, relative clauses, if-conditionals, direct↔reported speech, active↔passive, inversion / emphasis structures.
- **Comprehension is ONE passage** of 500–600 words, narrative or non-narrative, with ~10 OE questions totalling 20 marks. (This is contrary to some older or speculative summaries that describe two passages.)
- Of the 90 marks, **45 are constructed-response** (Comprehension Cloze 15 + Synthesis & Transformation 10 + Comprehension OE 20). No options, no word bank.

---

## Paper 3 — Listening Comprehension (20 marks)

- 20 MCQ items, 1 mark each.
- Audio types: conversations, announcements, instructions, narratives, short talks.
- Played by examiner; students answer on a separate answer sheet.

---

## Paper 4 — Oral Communication (40 marks) — 2025 reforms

### Reading Aloud — 15 marks

- Read a ~150-word passage aloud to the examiner.
- Preparation time: ~10 minutes.
- **New in 2025:** a short **preamble** precedes the passage, framing purpose / audience / context.
- **PACT framework** (Purpose, Audience, Context, Tone) is the recommended student approach.
- Marking strands: pronunciation, fluency & pace, expression & stress (proportions adjusted upward from the pre-2025 10-mark version).

### Stimulus-Based Conversation (SBC) — 25 marks

- Respond to questions about a visual stimulus.
- **New in 2025:** stimuli are **real-life photographs**, not drawings or static posters.
- Reading Aloud and SBC are **no longer thematically linked**: the passage and the picture are independent.
- Marking strands: communication of ideas, vocabulary, language accuracy (proportions adjusted upward from the pre-2025 20-mark version).
- Conversation duration: ~5–7 minutes.

## What changed in 2025 (summary, for downstream KB editors)

| Change | Pre-2025 | 2025+ |
|---|---|---|
| Total marks | 200 | 200 (unchanged) |
| Paper 1 marks | 55 | **50** (−5) |
| Paper 1 Situational | 15 | **14** (−1) |
| Paper 1 Continuous | 40 | **36** (−4) |
| Paper 1 new genre | — | **Article writing** added |
| Paper 1 Situational content points | 6 (all in stimulus) | 6 (**one is critical-thinking, not in stimulus**) |
| Paper 4 marks | 30 | **40** (+10) |
| Paper 4 Reading Aloud | 10 | **15** (+5; **PACT** framework, new preamble) |
| Paper 4 SBC | 20 | **25** (+5; **real-life photo** stimuli; no longer thematically linked to Reading Aloud) |
| Paper 2 marks | 95 | **90** (−5; reweighting within booklets) |
| Paper 3 marks | 20 | 20 (unchanged) |
| Synthesis & Transformation | present | **retained** (10 marks) |
| Comprehension OE structure | one passage | **one passage** (unchanged) |
