Paper 1 Simulator

Full Paper 1 in one sitting. Fifteen minutes for Situational Writing (14 marks), fifty-five minutes for Continuous Writing (36 marks — the picture is optional, you may include it or set your own scene). The timer auto-advances. Drafts auto-save every ten seconds, so you can reload safely. At the end you get a single-page Examiner scorecard across both sections. No model answers shown until you click Reveal mentor.

No Gemini API key set. The drafts are still saved, but the Examiner pass at the end needs a key. Add one in How to use and reload.
