Word Lab

Paste any word you've come across — in a passage, a book, a conversation. The Lab generates an AL1-calibrated vocab card: precise definition, part of speech, frequency tag, a sentence template, two example sentences, common collocations, and a paired show-don't-tell example for the F.A.S.T. drawer. Save the card to your local SRS bank, then drill it later.
