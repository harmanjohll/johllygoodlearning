How to use this

A short, honest guide for parents and students. 5 minutes to read.

What this is

A free, browser-based studio for a Primary 6 student in Singapore aiming for AL1 in PSLE English Language. It mirrors the existing Science Lab and Malay Studio. No login. No fees. No tracking. Everything stays in your browser.

The full kit

The studio has 25+ surfaces, organised on the hub into five tiles:

  • Start here — Compass (mood check-in + session plan), Daily 10 (10-min mixed test, streak-tracked), PSLE 2026 Countdown (20-week plan).
  • Write & speak — Composition Builder (Examiner / Coach / Annotator), Paper 1 Simulator, Paper 2 Mini, Listening Practice, Reading Aloud (MediaRecorder), SBC Practice.
  • Section sprints — Editing, Synthesis, Grammar Cloze, Comp Cloze, Visual Text, Comp OE. One deep drill per PSLE section.
  • Drill & vocab — Idioms SRS (SM-2 spaced), Word Lab (paste any word, build a card), Collocations, Connectors, Review queue.
  • See & remember — Mirror (cross-site mastery, paper readiness, mismatch nudge, wellbeing pulse), Mega Map (with Bridges toggle), Mentor Essays, Cheat Cards (per-paper + per-topic), My Bank (saved annotated essays + word cards).

Set up (2 minutes)

  1. Click Settings in the top right of any page.
  2. Get a free Google AI Studio API key ↗. Sign in with any Google account; no credit card needed.
  3. Paste the key. Click Test Key, then Save Key.
  4. The same key unlocks AI features in /lab/ and /malay/ too.

Without a key, most things still work — only the AI marker, AI Coach, AI generators, and the in-studio quiz generator need it.

A good daily loop

  1. Open Compass. One question check-in; it picks a topic by mood and routes you to the right mode.
  2. Take Daily 10. Ten mixed items drawn from all topics, weighted toward your weakest. Aim for ≥7/10 to keep the streak alive.
  3. Five idioms in SRS. Quick, low-friction.
  4. One sprint or simulator. Section sprint on weekdays; full P1 or P2 simulator on weekends.
  5. End at the Mirror. Read the "One honest nudge". Note your wellbeing tile if it's low or strong.

What's on every topic page

  • Learn tab — Quick Recap (from the Cheat Card) at the top, then key questions, glossary, lesson, common traps, mastery checkpoints, PSLE tips, summary.
  • Drill tab — 10–12 MCQ items with instant feedback, retry-wrong loop, 60-sec timer toggle.
  • Quiz tab — static curated quiz + AI quiz toggle (generates fresh items at the topic's level).
  • Mind Map tab — write your own one-line take on each key concept for this topic. Save when ≥3 are noted; earns +20 mastery on the topic.

AI generators (when you've finished the curated set)

Nine surfaces generate fresh practice material on demand:

  • Listening, Comp OE, SBC — new passage / stimulus with auto-marked questions.
  • Composition Builder — fresh prompt on a chosen genre.
  • Editing, Synthesis, Grammar Cloze, Comp Cloze, Visual Text — fresh items / passages.
  • Word Lab — paste any word; get a calibrated AL1 vocab card with template, examples, collocations, show-don't-tell pair.

All generations cache locally so a refresh keeps your current set.

The AL1 thesis

The gap between AL2 and AL1 in English is not about knowing more. It is about:

  1. Scientific language discipline. Fuzzy verbs (makes, gets, changes) become precise terms (photosynthesises, absorbs, condenses).
  2. Causal completeness in reasoning. AL3 says A and C; AL1 explains the mechanism B that connects them.
  3. Command-word literacy. Describe vs explain vs infer vs predict vs evaluate — each demands a different answer shape.
  4. Transfer. Novel passages don't rattle them, because they have internalised the principle, not the example.

Every feature in this studio (current and planned) serves one or more of these four.

For parents

This studio is designed to support a learner, not to replace tutoring or classroom teaching. Best used in 20-minute sessions, not marathon evenings. Showing up daily beats catching up on weekends. Rest days are honoured by the streak logic.

The Mirror page already includes a wellbeing pulse (14-day mood strip from Compass check-ins) and a cross-studio readout (Science / Malay / English overall mastery on the same screen). Nothing phones home — everything is local.

Privacy

No accounts. No server. No telemetry. Your study data lives only in your browser's localStorage. Clear your browser data and it is gone — this is by design. Your Gemini API key is the only thing leaving your device, and only to Google (when you trigger an AI feature).

Trouble?

  • AI says "API key not set": click Settings, paste your key, click Save.
  • AI says "quota exceeded": Google's free tier has daily limits. Wait until tomorrow, or create a second key.
  • Mind map won't save: write ≥3 one-line notes first; the save button unlocks when you cross the threshold.
  • Streak reset: the never-miss-twice rule kicks in if you skip two days running. The Compass offers rest days proactively after 5 consecutive days.
